Our Service

1. Job Posting & Management.

Employers can easily post new job opportunities, manage their listings, and track application progress through a user-friendly dashboard.

2. Job Search & Filtering.

Jobseekers can search for jobs based on category, location, job type, and experience level. Advanced filtering makes it easier to find the perfect job.

3. Resume Upload

Candidates can upload their resumes

4. Application Tracking

Users can monitor the status of their applications in real time, receiving updates when shortlisted, accepted, or rejected.

5. Job Alerts & Notifications

Get notified when new jobs matching your profile are posted. Email and in-platform notifications ensure you never miss an opportunity.

6. Feedback & Support

UUsers can send feedback or report issues for a better user experience. Our support team is here to assist with any queries.

7. Job Analytics (for Employers)

View performance reports on each job post, including number of views, applications received, and response rates.

8. Approval System (for Admins)

Admins can review and approve job posts before publishing, ensuring only quality opportunities appear on the platform.

9. Secure User Management

All users, whether jobseekers, employers, or admins, can manage their profiles securely with options to update details and reset passwords.
